Why I'm Taking This Course

I'm keen on refreshing my knowledge of HTML5 and CSS. I have been working in technology for several years now. One of my biggest academic achievement, was a Masters in Software Engineering back in 2016. Lately I have been exploring the realm of AI. I do think it will be the next big thing; if isn't already. I'm hoping this class will provide more depth and structure than what I could achieve by self-studying or watching tutorials in platforms such as LinkedIn Learning, MasterClass, and YouTube. There was a time when I was deeply involved in website development, but I shifted gears to back-end work and have since felt somewhat disconnected from the latest in front-end development. The landscape has certainly evolved, with many now favoring frameworks for their speed and efficiency in development. Despite this, I believe it's crucial to get back to the basics and solidify those foundational skills.

What I think about using ChatGPT for Research

I belive ChatGPT is a ground breaking technology as welll as a double-edged sword. It's super handy, efficient towards offering quick answers and help when you need it. But, there's that tricky part where it's easy to get a bit too comfy with it, right? We might lean on it more than we should and miss out on digging deeper into stuff ourselves. In way, I believe it can diminish one's ability to skills such as critical thinking and problem-solving skills could diminish Looking ahead, I envision a future where AI, particularly chatbots, integrates with virtual reality to create immersive work place and educational environments. Imagine a virtual classroom where students gather to learn, where a chatbot serve as a tutor or facilitator, offering personalized assistance, answering questions, and guiding discussions 24/7. The unfortunate side of things is that I believe AI will significantly transform the job market, displacing many roles that currently rely on routine or predictable tasks.
