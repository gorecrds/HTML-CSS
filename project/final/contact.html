About Me

Hello! I am Igor, and I have spent the last 15 years deeply involved in the tech industry. Beyond my professional life, I have a strong passion for investing, which I actively pursue in my downtime. I also hold a real estate license and work part-time helping friends and family navigate the real estate market.

I decided to center my project around real estate because it represents a key part of the American dream. Despite its allure, many people do not fully understand the intricacies, risks, and costs associated with owning property. Over the years, real estate has been seen not only as a sound investment but also as a way to hedge against inflation. However, in our current global economy, real estate competes with other investments like cryptocurrencies and stocks, which offer higher returns but come with increased risks. It is a classic scenario of high risk, high reward. Through this website, I aim to demystify the complexities of real estate investment and offer valuable insights that can guide you towards making confident and informed decisions in this competitive and ever-evolving market.
